Our Story

Vinyl Record House began as a small dream in a Tsim Sha Tsui shop, born from an insatiable passion for the warm, authentic sound that only vinyl can deliver.

Welcome to our premier online vinyl store, your ultimate destination for high-quality records! Our carefully curated collection features new releases, rare pressings, and timeless classics, all at competitive prices. With secure packaging, fast shipping, and exceptional customer service, we ensure your vinyl arrives in perfect condition. Dive into the rich, warm sound of vinyl and rediscover music the way it was meant to be heard. Happy spinning!

What started as a personal collection of rare finds and beloved classics has grown into a thriving community hub for music lovers across Hong Kong. We believe that every record tells a story, and every spin creates a memory.

Today, we're proud to be Hong Kong's trusted source for both timeless classics and the latest releases, serving collectors, casual listeners, and music enthusiasts from Central to New Territories.

What Makes Us Special

More than just a record store - we're a community

Curated Selection

Every record in our collection is hand-picked for quality and significance. From rare Cantopop gems to essential international listening, we ensure each piece meets our high standards.

Expert Knowledge

Our team consists of passionate music enthusiasts with decades of combined experience in Hong Kong's music scene. We're here to help you discover your next favorite album.

Community Focus

We host listening parties, artist meet-and-greets, and educational workshops in our Tsim Sha Tsui location. Music is meant to be shared, and we love bringing Hong Kong's music community together.

Our Mission

"To preserve and celebrate the art of vinyl records while creating a welcoming space where Hong Kong music lovers can discover, connect, and share their passion for authentic sound."

We're committed to supporting both established and emerging artists from Hong Kong, Asia, and around the world, promoting the vinyl format, and ensuring that future generations can experience the unmatched warmth and depth of analog sound.
